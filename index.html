How do we help your company?

About Best Workers

Best Workers offers premier recruitment services at no cost to you. We specialize in sourcing highly skilled bilingual freelancers from our extensive talent pool, tailored to meet your company's specific needs. Each freelancer undergoes rigorous qualification processes to ensure compatibility and increase the likelihood of success within your organization. Additionally, we provide comprehensive human resources support for the selected freelancer, streamlining the onboarding process for your convenience.

How do we filter our
freelancers?

At Best Workers, freelancers undergo a meticulous screening process before joining our talent pool. We rigorously evaluate their skills and backgrounds to ensure they align with our high standards. Only those who meet the initial criteria proceed to comprehensive assessments, including personality and English proficiency tests, as well as a video presentation detailing their professional journey.

We also offer tailored assessments to match company needs, streamlining recruitment and presenting the most suitable candidates efficiently. This saves time by bypassing resume reviews and interviews, ensuring a more effective hiring process.

You will not spend
money or time!

With Best Workers, you won't need to pay upfront fees to hire a freelancer. Instead, we deduct a percentage from the freelancer's salary each month. This saves you money while ensuring a cost-effective staffing solution. Additionally, you'll pay the freelancer's salary to Best Workers, and we'll handle the disbursement to the freelancer.
