How we help your company

About Best Workers

Best Workers offers premier recruitment services at no cost to you. We specialize in sourcing highly skilled bilingual freelancers from our extensive talent pool, tailored to meet your company's specific needs. Each freelancer undergoes rigorous qualification processes to ensure compatibility and increase the likelihood of success within your organization. Additionally, we provide comprehensive human resources support for the selected freelancer, streamlining the onboarding process for your convenience.

How we filter our
freelancers?

Prior to inclusion in our talent pool, each freelancer undergoes a meticulous screening process at Best Workers. We meticulously evaluate their skills and professional backgrounds to ensure alignment with our high standards. Only candidates who meet our initial criteria proceed to the next phase, where they are subjected to a battery of comprehensive assessments.

These assessments include a personality evaluation utilizing the renowned 16 Personalities test, as well as rigorous English language proficiency assessments such as the EFSET or Oxford test. Furthermore, freelancers are required to create a video presentation detailing their professional journey and past experiences.

In addition to these standardized evaluations, we offer the flexibility to tailor assessments according to the specific needs of your company and the job opening in question. This personalized approach streamlines the recruitment process, saving you valuable time and effort by presenting only the most suitable candidates for your consideration. By leveraging our comprehensive screening procedures, you can bypass the arduous task of sifting through resumes and conducting interviews, ensuring a more efficient and productive hiring process.

You will not expend
money nor time!

With Best Workers, you won't need to pay upfront fees to hire a freelancer. Instead, we deduct a percentage from the freelancer's salary each month. This saves you money while ensuring a cost-effective staffing solution. Additionally, you'll pay the freelancer's salary to Best Workers, and we'll handle the disbursement to the freelancer.
